# Approved Research Sources — BabyMania Organic System

Purpose:
Provide reliable, medically-aligned reference sources that the Blog Writer (Agent 04) can cite when explaining parenting, sleep, bath, skin, or safety guidance.

These sources are not mandatory citations.
They are trusted references used to strengthen credibility (E-E-A-T) when relevant.

## Usage Guidelines

When writing informational explanations, Agent 04 may use phrasing such as:

"According to pediatric guidelines…"

"Pediatric research suggests…"

"Studies on infant sleep indicate…"

"Pediatricians generally recommend…"

Direct linking to research papers is not required.

Sources should be used to strengthen explanations, not dominate the article.

Product recommendations must always remain editorial and natural.
